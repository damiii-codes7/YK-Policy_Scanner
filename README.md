# AI Legal Summarizer - https://policyguard-riskmanagementanalysis.streamlit.app/

An AI-powered legal-tech tool designed to simplify legal research by converting lengthy court judgments into concise, structured summaries.

## Features

* Upload legal judgments or case documents
* Generate simplified summaries instantly
* Extract key legal issues, facts, and rulings
* Improve readability for students, researchers, and legal professionals
* Reduce time spent reviewing lengthy case materials

## Why I Built This

Legal judgments are often long, complex, and difficult to process quickly. This project aims to make legal information more accessible by using AI-assisted summarization workflows to streamline legal research and understanding.

## Tech Stack

* Python
* Generative AI / LLM APIs
* Streamlit
